About the project

Pixel Store Fronts is an NFT collection created on the polygon network based on Japanese stores. The collection includes 2,500 unique randomly generated and stylistically curated pixelated stores.

Gas-Free Purchase

Polygon Blockchain is one of the most popular platforms for NFT marketplace development, also reduce transaction costs and enhances transaction speed.

0.01 ETH Starting Price

Whether you're just starting out in the NFT market or have a large collection, this is an affordable collection for you. The first 100 Pixel Stores NFTs are listed at a starting price of only 0.01 Polygon Ethereum.

Available on OpenSea.io

The collection is only available on the largest NFT marketplace. Go to the Pixel Store Fronts page on the OpenSea.io and buy the one you like.

Some Features

What makes each store special
Roof
There are more than twenty different types of roofs. Some traditional and others more modern. Variations of colors, vegetation and different objects are included.
Sign
The signs are based on real stores, together with the other parts of the store, they make each image unique.
Door and Tent
Tents are another important part of Japanese stores. There are twelve different types in total. While the doors have a great variation in details. So you can choose between a traditional ramen facade or a convenience store.
Types of trees
Maple
There are hundreds of Japanese maple varieties that come in various sizes with a large assortment of leaf shapes and colors that range from shades of green to orange, red and variegated. The most special NFTs include some of these trees.
Cherry Blossom (Sakura)
Cherry blossoms are a symbolic flower of the spring, a time of renewal, and the fleeting nature of life. You'll be lucky if you can get a store with this tree.
